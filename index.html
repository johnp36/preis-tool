Why This Exists

Most companies overpay vendors. Not because they lack intelligence — because they lack time, leverage, and negotiation expertise at the moment it matters.

Procurement teams at large companies negotiate thousands of contracts a year and build institutional knowledge most businesses never access. This product closes that gap.

It gives any operator the ability to walk into a vendor negotiation with the same calibrated strategy a seasoned procurement leader would use — without hiring one.
